About the Session

This session is crafted as a nervous system reset: we combine breath-led yoga to release tension, Contrology-style Pilates to note and restore alignment and functional strength, and a herbal talk to bring the senses back to the relationship with plants.

Session Goals

  • Regulate the nervous system through breathwork and gentle movement
  • Improve posture and core engagement with alignment-focused exercises
  • Introduce approachable herbal remedies and mindful tea drinking

Full Itinerary

Designed to be approachable and restorative.

  1. 7:00 — Grounding Yoga Flow (45 min): gentle pranayama, joint warm-ups, slow sun salutations and long-held restorative poses.
  2. 7:45 — Short break (10 min): mindful water and breath.
  3. 8:00 — Contrology: Posture & Alignment (60 min): pilates-style exercises focusing on core, pelvic placement and neutral spine with small props & mindful cueing.
  4. 9:00 — Herbs & Tea (30 min): short talk, tea tasting, and mindful sipping followed by a closing intention practice.
  5. 9:30 — Stillness Nest / Journaling (30 min): optional quiet time — sit, reflect, or try a guided 10-min body scan.
